Put the verb in the correct form,present perfect or present perfect continuous.

1.You meet Kate as she is leaving the swimming pool.

You say:Hi,Kate. _________ (you / swim?)

2.You have arrived a little late to meet Ben who is waiting for you.

You say:I’m sorry I’m late,Ben. ______ (you/wait/long?)

3.Jane’s little boy comes into the house with a very dirty face and dirty hands.

His mother says:Why are you so dirty? _________ (what/you/do?)

4.You are in a shop and see Anna. You didn’t know she worked there.

You say:Hi, Anna. _________ (how long/you/work/here?)

5.A friend tells you about his job – he sells phones.

You say:You sell phones? ________ (how long/you/do/that?)

6.It’s raining.The rain started two hours ago.It _________ (rain) for two hours.

7.We are waiting for the bus.We started waiting 20 minutes ago.We _________ (wait) for 20 minutes.

8.I’m learning Japanese.I started classes in December.I _________ (learn) Japanese since December.

9.Jessica is working in a hotel.She started working there on 18 January.She _________ (work) since 18 January.

10.Our friends always go to Italy for their holidays.The first time was years ago.They_________  (go) there for years.

11.Look!Somebody __________ (break) that window.

12.You look tired. ________ (you/work) hard?

13. ________ (you/ever/work) in a factory?No, never.

14.Where’s Lisa?Where _________ (she/go)?

15.This is a very old book.I  _________ (have) it since I was a child.

16.Have you been busy?No, I _________ (watch) TV.

17.My brother is an actor.He ________ (appear) in several films.

18.Sorry I’m late.That’s all right.I _________ (not/wait) long.

19.Are you OK? You look as if you ________ (cry).

20.Is it still raining?No,it ________ (stop).

21.The children are tired now.They ________ (play) in the garden.

22.I _________ (lose) my phone. _________ (you/see) it?

23.I __________ (read) the book you lent me,but I _________ (not/finish) it yet.It’s really interesting.

24.I _________ (read) the book you lent me,so you can have it back now.

25.Tom started reading a book two hours ago.He is still reading it and now he is on page 53.He _______ (read) for two hours.
